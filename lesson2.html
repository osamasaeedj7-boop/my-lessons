Common Mistakes from Last Lesson

I'm relaxing.
I feel relaxed.
This is my dreams.

Section B

Sentence-Building Patterns

Pattern 1: Job

I am a/an…

I work for…

I work in…

"I am a civil engineer."

"I work for a private company."

"I work in engineering."

Pattern 2: Likes and Hobbies

I like…

I enjoy…

In my free time, I…

"I enjoy creating fashion reels."

"In my free time, I play the guitar."

"I like walking in the park."

Section B

Sentence-Building Patterns

Pattern 3: Feelings

I feel…

This makes me feel…

"I feel relaxed."

"This makes me feel happy."

"I feel satisfied when people like my work."

Pattern 4: Opinions

I think… because…

In my opinion,…

For example,…

"I think teachers are very important because they guide students."

"In my opinion, social media influences people a lot."

"For example, Instagram affects fashion choices."

Section C

How to Make a Better Answer

Weak Answer

I like my job.

Better Answer

I like my job because it has always been my dream. I enjoy architecture and design. For example, I feel happy when I help people improve their homes.

The Formula

Answer Reason Example

Section D

Focus on One Idea

Question

What do you do in your free time?

Weak Answer

I play guitar, swim, walk, use Instagram, make reels, read stories, watch TV…

Better Answer

In my free time, I mostly enjoy creating reels for Instagram. I like choosing outfits and filming videos. It is enjoyable because it helps me express my style and creativity.

Golden Rule

One main idea is better than many small ideas.

Section E

How to Avoid Repetition

Repetitive Answer

I like my job. I like this job. This job is good. I am happy in this job.

Improved Answer

I like my job because it combines creativity and technical skill. For example, when a client is happy with my work, I feel satisfied.

Replace repetition with:

Reason Example Feeling Result

Section F

What to Do if You Do Not Understand

1

Ask politely:

"Sorry, could you repeat the question, please?"

"Sorry, I didn’t catch that."

2

Listen again for key words.

3

Answer the general topic.

Example Question:

How has social media influenced people?

Possible Answer:

"I think social media affects people a lot today. For example, many people follow fashion, design, and lifestyle content online."

Section G

Part 3 Answer Structure

Best Simple Structure

I think… because… For example…

Example 1

Do teachers strongly influence students’ lives?

I think teachers strongly influence students’ lives because they guide students and help them learn. For example, a good teacher can inspire a student to study harder.

Section G

Part 3 Answer Structure

Example 2

Is influence always positive?

I don’t think influence is always positive because some people can copy bad behavior. For example, social media can sometimes influence people in negative ways.
