Quick Speaking Check

Part 1

Do you prefer studying alone or with other people?

Part 3

Rule:

• Speak naturally.

• Do not worry about mistakes.

• This is only a short check.

Skill 1: Extend Your Answer

Strong IELTS answers are not only one sentence.

Answer Reason Example Result

Example Question

Do you prefer studying alone or with other people?

Weak Answer

I prefer studying alone.

Better Answer

I usually prefer studying alone because I can focus better. For example, when I study by myself, I can organize my time more easily. As a result, I often learn faster.

Practice: Extend Your Answer

Use this structure:

Answer Reason Example Result

Questions:

  1. Do you prefer studying in the morning or at night?
  2. Do you enjoy learning English? Why?

Useful starters:

I prefer… I enjoy… because… for example… as a result…

Skill 2: Past / Present / Future

This is a highly useful IELTS speaking skill to show grammar range.

In the past…

Nowadays…

In the future…

Example Question

How has your English changed over time?

Model Answer

In the past, my English was weaker and I felt shy. Nowadays, I can speak more and understand more vocabulary. In the future, I want to become more confident and speak more naturally.

Practice: Past / Present / Future

Try to answer using:

In the past… / Nowadays… / In the future…

Questions:

1

How has your life changed in the last few years?

2

How do you think your future will be different after studying abroad?

Part 2: New Topic

Describe a skill you would like to improve

You should say:

  • what the skill is
  • why you want to improve it
  • how you are trying to improve it
  • how this skill can help you in the future

Part 2 structure:

  1. What the skill is
  2. Why it is important
  3. What I am doing now
  4. How it will help me
  5. How I feel about it

Useful phrases:

I’d like to improve… It is important for me because… Right now, I am trying to… This skill will help me… In the future, I hope to… I feel motivated because…

Part 2 Model Answer

Topic: Describe a skill you would like to improve

I’d like to improve my English speaking skill. It is important for me because I want to study in Italy and do well in the IELTS exam. Right now, I am practicing speaking with my teacher and trying to learn useful vocabulary. This skill will help me in my studies and future career. I feel motivated because I know it can change my future.

Part 3: Better Structure

Part 3 answers need shape and deep development.

Use this formula:

Opinion Reason Example Final sentence

Easy form:

  • • I think…
  • • One reason is that…
  • • For example,…
  • • That’s why…

Model Question

Why do some people learn skills faster than others?

Model Answer

I think some people learn skills faster than others because they practice more regularly. For example, a person who studies every day usually improves faster than someone who studies only once a week. That’s why discipline is very important in learning.

Part 3 Practice

Answer these questions using:

Opinion Reason Example Final sentence

Questions:

1

Why do some people learn skills faster than others?

2

Has technology made learning easier?

3

Do you think practical skills are important for young people?

Useful Vocabulary

skill = beceri

Ex: Speaking is an important skill for IELTS.

improve = geliştirmek

Ex: I want to improve my speaking.

practice = pratik yapmak

Ex: Regular practice helps people improve faster.

confident = kendinden emin

Ex: I want to feel more confident when I speak.

future = gelecek

Ex: This skill can help me in the future.

opportunity = fırsat

Ex: Studying abroad is a good opportunity for me.

regularly = düzenli olarak

Ex: People improve faster when they practice regularly.

effective = etkili

Ex: This is an effective way to learn English.

focus = odaklanmak

Ex: I can focus better when I study alone.

challenge = zorluk

Ex: Speaking English is a challenge for me, but I want to improve.

Useful Speaking Phrases

Well, I’d say… In my opinion… One reason is that… For example, … As a result, … In the future, … Right now, I am trying to… This will help me because… That’s why…
